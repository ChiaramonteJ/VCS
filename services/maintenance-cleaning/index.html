What Is Maintenance Cleaning?

Maintenance cleaning is designed to preserve results after a deep clean or to keep an already-maintained home consistently clean over time.

It targets essential recurring tasks so your space stays manageable and welcoming week after week.

Popular Add-Ons

  • Appliance detailing at selected intervals
  • Laundry support for linens and clothes
  • Mini deep-clean rotation by room
  • Pet-hair focused maintenance areas
  • Seasonal deep-clean booster visits

Maintenance Cleaning Checklist

Core Surfaces

  • Dusting and wipe-downs
  • High-touch sanitizing focus
  • Visual reset of shared areas
  • Spot cleanup

Floor Care

  • Vacuuming throughout
  • Mopping hard floors
  • Edge and corner attention
  • Entryway refresh

Bathrooms

  • Sink and vanity cleaning
  • Toilet cleaning
  • Mirror and fixture polish
  • Floor and touchpoint care

Kitchen

  • Counter and sink cleaning
  • Exterior appliance wipe
  • Quick backsplash refresh
  • Floor vacuum/mop

Service Notes

  • Works best on a weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly cadence
  • Maintains cleanliness after initial deep-clean baseline
  • Scope can prioritize your high-use rooms
  • Ideal for reducing weekend catch-up chores
