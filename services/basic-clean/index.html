What Is Included in a Basic Clean?

Our Basic Clean is designed for regular upkeep. It focuses on the tasks that make the biggest difference in how your home looks and feels day to day.

This is a great choice for busy households that want consistent cleanliness without a full deep-clean scope each visit.

Best For

  • Weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly upkeep
  • Homes already in generally maintained condition
  • Busy families and working professionals
  • Reducing daily cleaning stress
  • Pairing with occasional deep-clean add-ons

Basic Clean Checklist

Living Areas

  • Dust reachable surfaces
  • Vacuum carpets and rugs
  • Mop hard floors
  • General straightening

Bedrooms

  • Dust furniture surfaces
  • Vacuum or mop floors
  • Tidy visible surfaces
  • Empty small trash bins

Bathrooms

  • Clean sinks and counters
  • Clean toilet exterior and bowl
  • Wipe mirrors and fixtures
  • Mop bathroom floors

Kitchen

  • Wipe counters and backsplash
  • Clean exterior appliance surfaces
  • Clean sink and faucet
  • Vacuum/mop kitchen floor

Popular Add-Ons

  • Inside fridge or oven cleaning
  • Laundry wash, dry, and fold
  • Extra attention for pet hair areas
  • Mini deep clean for bathrooms or kitchen
  • Move-in or move-out prep add-ons

Service Notes

  • Scope is customized to your home and priorities
  • Pricing is based on home size, condition, and frequency
  • Initial visits may require more time than maintenance visits
  • We can recommend recurring cadence after your first quote
