What Is a Mini Deep Clean?

Mini deep clean is designed for clients who need extra detail in specific areas, without booking a full-home deep clean.

It is ideal for kitchens, bathrooms, and high-use spaces where buildup happens faster.

Popular Add-Ons

  • Add onto recurring Basic or Maintenance cleanings
  • Pair with appliance detailing for kitchen resets
  • Use for pre-event room touch-ups
  • Rotate focus rooms month to month
  • Combine with laundry service for full refresh

Mini Deep Clean Focus

Kitchen Priority

  • Grease-prone surface detailing
  • Backsplash deep wipe
  • Sink and fixture polish
  • Targeted floor edge cleaning

Bathroom Priority

  • Soap scum treatment
  • Mirror and fixture shine
  • Detailed sink/toilet area work
  • Corner and base cleaning

High-Touch Zones

  • Switches and handles
  • Door and trim spots
  • Frequently used surfaces
  • Visible buildup removal

Floor Refresh

  • Detailed vacuum pass
  • Spot treatment
  • Mop reset in target rooms
  • Edge-focused cleanup

Service Notes

  • Scope is room-priority driven
  • Can be scheduled one-time or recurring
  • Useful for ongoing buildup prevention in high-use zones
