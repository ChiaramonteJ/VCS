Why Choose Bi-Weekly Cleaning?

Bi-weekly service is one of the most popular schedules for homes that need regular support but do not require weekly visits.

It provides reliable upkeep for kitchens, bathrooms, floors, and living spaces while keeping effort manageable between visits.

Popular Add-Ons

  • Appliance detailing add-ons
  • Laundry service support
  • Mini deep-clean focus zones
  • Pet hair heavy-area attention
  • Occasional deep-clean resets
